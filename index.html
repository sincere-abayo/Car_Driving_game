How to Play

Objective: Reach the Magic Garden through 3 challenging stages!

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys: Steer left/right
  • Spacebar: Brake
  • F: Toggle Free Ride Mode
  • R: Toggle Radio Music
  • ESC: Pause/Menu

Tips: Avoid AI cars and obstacles. Each stage gets harder!
